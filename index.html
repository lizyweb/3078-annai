About Us

Annai Theresa Pre - Primary Educationl Charitable Trust

Established in 2006, Annai Theresa Pre - Primary Educational Charitable Trust is a non-profit organization committed to uplifting the lives of rural communities through comprehensive support in health, education, and skill development. Our focus is on empowering individuals, particularly women and children, to create a sustainable and prosperous future.

Programmes

Discover our diverse programs at Annai Theresa Pre - Primary Educational Charitable Trust, including health, education, child welfare, and women development, fostering community growth and empowerment.

Health Initiatives

Regular health camps and medical check-ups.
Health awareness programs on nutrition, hygiene, and preventive care.
Mobile health clinics to reach remote areas.

Educational Programs

Tuition centers providing after-school support to students.
Establishment and support of nursery schools for early childhood education.
Scholarships and educational materials for underprivileged students.

Child Welfare

Nutritional support programs for children.
Safe spaces and recreational activities for holistic development.
Counselling and support services for vulnerable children.

Women Development

Free tailoring and computer training courses.
Skill training workshops in areas such as handicrafts, entrepreneurship, and financial literacy.
Initiatives to promote self-employment and small business development for women.

Skill Training

Vocational training programs in various trades such as carpentry, electrical work, and sewing.
Computer literacy and IT skills training.
Life skills and job readiness workshops.

Community Development Initiatives

Infrastructure development projects such as clean water supply and sanitation facilities.
Environmental conservation programs.
Community mobilization and leadership training.
